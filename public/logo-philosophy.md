# Vectorial Compression

A philosophy of distilling complex systems into singular, charged forms. Where sprawling networks collapse into a single decisive gesture. The aesthetic canon of reduction without loss — every cut reveals rather than removes, leaving behind only the essential voltage of an idea.

Space is treated as negative pressure. Dark fields do not recede; they hold tension, amplify the luminosity of what remains. The palette is disciplined: near-black foundations, a single chromatic current running indigo-to-cyan, white used only as a crystalline accent — never for decoration, only for clarification. Color speaks in gradients that suggest motion, data flow, and traversal.

Form follows the logic of connection. Geometric units — arcs, rectangles, nodes — are locked in precise relational geometry. The interlocking of forms is never decorative; it is structural, implying system, pathway, continuity. Two objects in alignment become a third, invisible object: the relationship between them. This invisible element is the true subject of the work.

Scale and weight are instruments of meaning. Thick strokes carry load, thin strokes carry intelligence. The composition never rests at equilibrium — there is always a dominant axis, a visual current the eye follows from entry to anchor. Typography enters as a whisper: thin-weight, tracked out, lowercase — not text but texture, identity encoded at the threshold of legibility.

This is work that rewards patience. What appears simple at distance reveals, on approach, a system of deliberate choices — corner radii calculated to fractions, gradient angles aligned to underlying geometry, spacing derived from the same unit that governs every other measurement. Meticulously crafted. The product of deep expertise. Every pixel placed as if the composition would be examined under glass.
